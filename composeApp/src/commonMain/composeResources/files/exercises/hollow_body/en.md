#### Exercise Description
Hollow Body is an isometric core exercise that strengthens the rectus abdominis and deep stabilizing muscles of the spine. It involves holding a tense, hollow position with the lower back pressed into the floor.

#### Tips
- Lie on your back and press your lower back into the floor.
- Lift your legs and shoulders off the ground simultaneously.
- Keep your abdominal muscles tightly engaged throughout.
- Extend your arms overhead or keep them alongside your body.
- Breathe steadily – do not hold your breath.
- Start with a regression (e.g., bent knees) if needed.

#### Common Mistakes
- Allowing the lower back to arch off the floor.
- Lifting limbs too high and losing control.
- Lack of core engagement.
- Holding your breath.
- Progressing to a full version too early.
- Relaxing the body during the hold.
