#### Exercise Description
Cobra Stretch is a yoga-based stretch that focuses on elongating the abdominal muscles while opening the chest. It also strengthens the lower back and improves spinal mobility, especially in the lumbar region.

#### Tips
- Lie face down with your hands placed under your shoulders.
- Slowly lift your chest by partially or fully extending your arms.
- Keep your hips and legs in contact with the ground.
- Open your chest and draw your shoulder blades down and back.
- Keep your shoulders away from your ears.
- Breathe slowly and with control.

#### Common Mistakes
- Overarching the lower back causing discomfort.
- Relying too much on the arms instead of engaging the back muscles.
- Lifting the hips off the ground.
- Tensing the neck and throwing the head too far back.
- Moving too quickly without control.
