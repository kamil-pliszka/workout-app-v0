#### Exercise Description
Side Plank Left is an isometric exercise that strengthens the oblique abdominal muscles, core, and lateral stabilizers of the torso. It involves holding a side support position on the left side while maintaining proper body alignment.

#### Tips
- Place your left elbow directly under your shoulder.
- Keep your body in a straight line from head to feet.
- Engage your core and glutes.
- Lift your hips and avoid letting them drop.
- Look forward or down to maintain neck stability.
- Breathe steadily and calmly.

#### Common Mistakes
- Letting the hips drop.
- Rotating the torso forward or backward.
- Lack of core and glute engagement.
- Excessive tension in the supporting shoulder.
- Holding your breath.
- Losing a straight body alignment.
