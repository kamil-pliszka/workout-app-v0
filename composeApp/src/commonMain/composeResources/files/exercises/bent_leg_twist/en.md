#### Exercise Description
Bent Leg Twist is a core exercise that primarily targets the abdominal muscles, especially the obliques. It involves controlled torso rotation with legs lifted and bent, increasing core tension and improving stability.

#### Tips
- Keep your core engaged throughout the entire movement.
- Perform the motion slowly and with control – avoid jerking.
- Maintain your legs bent at approximately a 90-degree angle.
- Breathe steadily – exhale during the twist.
- Keep your spine neutral, avoid rounding your back.

#### Common Mistakes
- Performing the movement too fast without control.
- Dropping the legs or losing core tension.
- Rounding the lower back.
- Using arms instead of rotating the torso.
- Holding your breath.
