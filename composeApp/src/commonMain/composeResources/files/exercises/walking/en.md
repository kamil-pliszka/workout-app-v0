#### Exercise Description
Walking is a basic, low-intensity physical activity that engages the whole body, especially the legs and glutes. Regular walking improves cardiovascular fitness, supports fat loss, and benefits joint and spine health.

#### Tips
- Maintain an upright and natural posture.
- Look forward instead of down.
- Step naturally from heel to toe.
- Keep your shoulders and arms relaxed and moving freely.
- Maintain a steady pace suitable for your fitness level.
- Breathe calmly and consistently.

#### Common Mistakes
- Slouching or leaning forward.
- Stiff or unnatural walking pattern.
- Not using arm movement.
- Walking too fast without proper control.
- Flat-footed stepping without smooth transition.
- Holding your breath.
