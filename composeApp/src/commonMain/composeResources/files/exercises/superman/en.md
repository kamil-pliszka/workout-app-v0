#### Exercise Description
Superman is a back-strengthening exercise that targets the lower back muscles, as well as the glutes and rear shoulders. It involves simultaneously lifting the arms and legs while lying face down, improving spinal stability and strength.

#### Tips
- Lie face down with legs straight and arms extended forward.
- Lift your arms, chest, and legs off the ground at the same time.
- Engage your core to support the spine.
- Keep your gaze downward to avoid neck strain.
- Move slowly and with control.
- Breathe steadily throughout the exercise.

#### Common Mistakes
- Lifting limbs too high without control.
- Straining the neck by looking up.
- Lack of core engagement.
- Using momentum instead of controlled movement.
- Holding your breath.
- Overarching the lower back.
