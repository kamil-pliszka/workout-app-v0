#### Exercise Description
Running is a fundamental aerobic activity that engages the whole body, especially the legs, glutes, and cardiovascular system. It improves endurance, fitness, and supports fat loss.

#### Tips
- Keep an upright but relaxed posture.
- Land on the midfoot rather than heavily on the heel.
- Maintain a rhythmic breathing pattern matching your pace.
- Relax your shoulders and avoid unnecessary tension in the arms.
- Use a natural, alternating arm swing.
- Increase intensity gradually.

#### Common Mistakes
- Poor posture and slouching.
- Heavy heel striking.
- Tension in shoulders, arms, and hands.
- Increasing pace too quickly without adaptation.
- Inconsistent or chaotic running rhythm.
- Holding your breath or irregular breathing.
