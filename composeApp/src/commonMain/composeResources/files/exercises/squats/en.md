#### Exercise Description
Squats are a fundamental strength exercise targeting the thighs, glutes, and core stabilizers. The movement involves controlled lowering and raising of the body through flexion and extension of the hips, knees, and ankles.

#### Tips
- Stand shoulder-width apart or slightly wider.
- Keep your back straight and core engaged.
- Track your knees in line with your toes.
- Lower yourself to a comfortable depth with control.
- Keep your weight distributed across your whole foot, especially heels and midfoot.
- Rise up with control and stability.

#### Common Mistakes
- Rounding the back or leaning forward excessively.
- Knees collapsing inward.
- Lifting heels off the ground.
- Using too shallow range of motion.
- Using momentum instead of control.
- Holding your breath.
