#### Exercise Description
Flutter Kicks are a core exercise that primarily targets the lower abdominal muscles and hip flexors. The movement involves alternating up-and-down leg motions while maintaining constant core tension.

#### Tips
- Lie on your back with your arms at your sides or under your hips.
- Lift your legs slightly off the ground.
- Perform alternating, quick but controlled leg movements.
- Press your lower back firmly into the floor.
- Keep your core engaged throughout the exercise.
- Breathe steadily – do not hold your breath.

#### Common Mistakes
- Lifting the lower back off the ground.
- Raising the legs too high instead of working in a low range.
- Moving too fast without control.
- Lack of core engagement.
- Bending the knees instead of keeping legs straight.
- Holding your breath.
