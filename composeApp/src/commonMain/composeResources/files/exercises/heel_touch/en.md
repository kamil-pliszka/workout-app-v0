#### Exercise Description
Heel Touch is a core exercise that strengthens the oblique abdominal muscles and improves torso control. It involves alternately reaching toward the heels while lying on your back with an engaged core.

#### Tips
- Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor.
- Slightly lift your upper back to engage your abs.
- Reach toward each heel alternately in a controlled motion.
- Move smoothly without jerking.
- Keep your core engaged throughout the exercise.
- Breathe rhythmically – exhale with each reach.

#### Common Mistakes
- Pulling the neck instead of engaging the abs.
- Performing the movement too quickly and without control.
- Fully relaxing the torso between reps.
- Lack of core engagement.
- Losing control of the lower back.
- Holding your breath.
